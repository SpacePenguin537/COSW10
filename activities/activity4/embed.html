Pinocchio-P

Pinocchio-P is a Japanese VOCALOID producer. He has made numerous hit songs using namely Hatsune Miku, but occassionally other character voicebanks such as Kagamine Rin and GUMI. The majority of his songs feature energetic and upbeat soundtracks, with deeper meanings behind the lyrics. Pinocchio-P is also the creater of the mascots Aimaina and Doushite-Chan.
